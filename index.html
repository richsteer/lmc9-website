La Manga Club



La Manga Club, is a 5 star award winning resort on the South coast of Spain.

The La Manga Club resort (map) is located 30mins south of Murcia Airport or 1hr south of Alicante Airport.

La Manga Club has 3 golf courses on the resort - The North, South and West courses.

All major UK airports fly to Murcia (RMU) or Alicante (ALC) and the flight time is 2h30m

The private beach "La Cala" with a resturant over looking the sea.

The Par 3 golf course, which backs onto the Las Palmeras swimming pool.

A wide choice of bars and resturants (map) are on the resort. Our picks are The Last Drop, La Barra, Dantes and El Bistro.

The tennis center has 28 tennis courts available to book, the courts are different surfaces.

More information about the La Manga Club resort can be found on the offical website here lamangaclub.com including the history behind the club.

The beutiful La Manga beaches and lagoon are a 20min drive away.

Las Palmeras 9



Las Palmeras 9 is a two bedroom villa, with ensuite to the master. In the Lounge/diner there is also sofa bed. Kitchen is well appointed and includes a breakfast bar. Outside there are three terraces.

Lounge/dining room has sofa bed, coffee table, dining table and TV with playstation. The patio doors lead to the main rear terrace with outside table, chairs and built-in barbeque.

The master bedroom with patio doors leading to a private terrace. It also has an en-suite bathroom with walk-in shower, bath, twin wash basins and toilet.

Bars, restaurants, Par 47 golf and tennis center only a short walk.

Top terrace for sun worshippers, the back terrace has BBQ and outdoor dining facilities.

View from the back terrace over La Manga strip and lagoon.

The fitted kitchen is well equipped with microwave, hub oven, fridge freezer, washing machine and coffee machine.

Anyone staying at Las Palmeras 9 benefits from "owners rates" for golf and tennis.
