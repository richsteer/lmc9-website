La Manga Club Resort

The central location makes Las Palmeras a good choice for easy access to the Par 47 golf course, Tennis Centre and a number of bars and restaurants.

La Manga Club, is an award winning resort on the South coast of Spain. It has 3 golf courses, 28 tennis courts, football and cricket centers. There's a five star hotel and over 15 resturants and bars catering for all tastes.

La Manga Club resort is located 30mins south of Murcia Airport or 1hr south of Alicante Airport - map here. It's also just 20mins from La Manga strip, with beaches on either side of the strip and Europe's largest lagoon.

More information about the La Manga Club resort can be found on the offical website here lamangaclub.com. Including details of facilities and the history behind the club.

Las Palmeras 9

The central location makes Las Palmeras a good choice for easy access to the Par 47 golf course, Tennis Centre and a number of bars and restaurants.

Lounge

The high ceiling lounge/dining room with exposed beams is comfortably furnished and complete with flat screen TV and playstation. Patio doors lead to the open terrace with table, chairs and built-in barbeque.

Master Bedroom

The master bedroom has a double bed and patio doors which lead to a private balcony. It also benefits from an en-suite bathroom with walk-in shower, bath tub, twin wash basins and toilet.

Bedroom 2

The second bedroom has twin beds and use of the separate shared bathroom with toilet, shower and sink.

Pool

The Las Palmeras swimming pool, all residents can use the pool to relax by the Par 48 pitch and putt course.

Kitchen

The fitted kitchen is well equipped with washing machine, microwave, oven, hob and extractor, fridge freezer and coffee machine.

Top Sun Terrace

Top terrace for sun worshippers, the back terrace has BBQ and outdoor dining facilities.
