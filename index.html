La Manga Club Resort

La Manga Club, is an award winning resort on the South east coast of Spain. Awards include “Spain’s Luxury Resort Spa“ and “Europe’s Leading Sports Resort“.

La Manga Club resort is 30minutes south of Murcia Airport or 1hr south of Alicante Airport and just 20mins from La Manga strip.

La Manga Club is a gated secure resort and made up of many “communities”, each community is a collection of villa’s/apartments with a community pool. Our Villa is in the centrally located La Palmeras community which overlooks the Par 42 pitch and putt course, it has a fabulous pool and is short walk to many restaurants and bars.
